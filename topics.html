Overview

The FIRST INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON MULTIDISCIPLINARY SOLUTIONS IN ENGINEERING & TECHNOLOGY (MSET 2025) will be organized by the Department of Engineering, Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University, Trichy, at the DSU Campus, Trichy, India, on April 23 & April 25 2025. The conference will feature invited talks and contributory papers covering a wide range of topics in engineering and technology.

Highlights

  • Keynote speeches by renowned experts
  • Best Paper Awards
  • Panel Discussions on Emerging Technologies
  • Workshops and Tutorials
  • Exhibition of Innovative Projects
