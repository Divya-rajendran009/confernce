About Us

The Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University (DSU) has been established under the Tamil Nadu Private Universities Act, 2019, located in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, India. Uniqueness of DSU lies in its multi-disciplinary nature in offering a wide range of academic programmes encompassing medicine and engineering. Our motto is "education for the real world" with dedication and commitment towards nurturing the future generation. Green ambience with state-of-the-art infrastructure along with top-class faculty aims to serve the need of national and international students.

About
School Of Engineering and Technology

School of Engineering and Technology (SET) in DSU is a beacon of innovation and academic excellence, offering cutting-edge programs designed to shape the next generation of engineering leaders. SET has a range of 12 departments that include Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Agriculture Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Biotechnology, Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence and Date Structure, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Cyber Security and Internet of Things. With a strong emphasis on practical learning and research, SET equips students with the technical expertise, critical thinking abilities, and hands-on experience needed to excel in a competitive, technology-driven world. SET boasts a team of highly qualified and experienced Faculty who are dedicated to mentoring students and fostering a culture of academic rigor and innovation. State-of-the-art laboratories, well-equipped workshops, and modern computing facilities ensure that students have access to the latest tools and technologies to enhance their learning experience. In addition to academics, SET emphasizes holistic development by encouraging participation in technical clubs, competitions, and industry collaborations. Through internships, industrial visits, and live projects, students gain real-world exposure and develop skills that align with industry standards. Memorandums of Understanding with renowned national and international institutions further enrich the learning experience, providing a platform for students to broaden their horizons. SET is committed to nurturing competent, ethical, and socially responsible engineers who are prepared to tackle real-world challenges and make meaningful contributions to the society.

Engage with experts, School of Engineering and Technology (SET) proudly hosts a two day International Conference on Multidisciplinary Solutions in Engineering and Technology, MSET 2025 on 25th and 26th April 2025. The Conference is a premier platform for global academics, researchers, industry professionals, and students to converge and exchange cutting-edge ideas. This conference is dedicated to addressing complex challenges through innovative, multidisciplinary approaches that bridge diverse fields of engineering and technology. The conference brings together experts from various domains, including mechanical, electrical, computer science, electronics, science, humanities and emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, cyber security, machine learning and IoT. By fostering collaboration across disciplines, the conference aims to explore novel solutions to pressing societal and industrial challenges. Key highlights of MSET 2025 include keynote speeches by renowned international and national experts, technical paper presentations, and panel discussions. Participants gain valuable insights into global trends, groundbreaking research, and practical applications that shape the future of engineering and technology.
